2 Required Footer Elements Checklist

Every single one of these MUST be present on ALL pages:

  • Privacy Policy Link: How customer data is collected, stored, and used
  • Terms & Conditions Link: Legal agreement between you and customers
  • Refund/Return Policy Link: Clear explanation of refund process and timeframes
  • Contact Information: Email, phone, and physical business address
  • Physical Address: Real street address (not P.O. Box) where business operates
  • FDA Disclaimer: Required for all supplement/health products
  • Results Disclaimer: "Results may vary" and testimonial disclaimer
  • Copyright Notice: ¬© Year, Company Name, All Rights Reserved
  • Affiliate Disclosure: If using affiliate links or partners
  • Medical Disclaimer: Not medical advice, consult physician

3 Required Legal Pages

You must create and link to these complete legal documents:

Legal Page Required Content Why It's Required
Privacy Policy Data collection, storage, usage, cookies, third-party sharing, GDPR/CCPA compliance Federal law (FTC) and state laws (CCPA). Payment processors require it.
Terms & Conditions User obligations, liability limitations, dispute resolution, jurisdiction, warranty disclaimers Legal protection for your business. Defines customer relationship.
Refund Policy Refund timeframe (60-day guarantee), process, conditions, return shipping, processing time FTC requires clear refund terms. Reduces chargeback risk.
Contact Page Support email, phone, hours, physical address, contact form Consumer protection laws require accessible contact method.
Warning: Don't use generic templates without customization. Your policies must accurately reflect YOUR business practices, or you're exposed to legal liability.

4 FDA Disclaimer (Supplements)

If you're selling supplements, health products, or anything making health claims, this disclaimer is MANDATORY:

Required FDA Disclaimer:

"These statements have not been evaluated by the Food and Drug Administration. This product is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease."

When FDA Disclaimer is Required:

  • Dietary supplements (vitamins, herbs, minerals)
  • Weight loss products
  • Health and wellness products
  • Beauty/anti-aging products making health claims
  • Any product claiming to affect body structure or function

Additional Medical Disclaimers:

"The information provided on this website is for informational purposes only and is not intended as a substitute for advice from your physician or other health care professional. You should not use the information on this website for diagnosis or treatment of any health problem. Consult with a healthcare professional before starting any diet, exercise, supplementation program, or if you have or suspect you might have a health problem."

5 Results & Testimonial Disclaimers

The FTC requires clear disclaimers when showing testimonials, before/after photos, or income claims:

Required Results Disclaimer:

"The testimonials and examples used are exceptional results and do not apply to the average purchaser. They are not intended to represent or guarantee that anyone will achieve the same or similar results. Each individual's success depends on his or her background, dedication, desire, and motivation. As with any health-related program or service, your results may vary, and will be based on many variables including but not limited to, your individual capacity, life experience, unique health and genetic profile, starting point, expertise, and level of commitment."

When Results Disclaimers are Required:

  • Before/after photos or testimonials
  • Weight loss claims with specific amounts
  • Income or earnings claims
  • Time-based results ("lose 20 lbs in 30 days")
  • Any specific outcome promises
FTC Enforcement: The FTC has fined companies millions for inadequate testimonial disclaimers. Don't risk it - include the full disclaimer.

8 Common Compliance Mistakes

These Mistakes Will Get Your Account Suspended:
  • ‚ùå No Footer on Upsell Pages: Every page needs full footer, not just main pages
  • ‚ùå Generic/Template Policies: Copy-paste policies that don't match your business
  • ‚ùå Broken Policy Links: Links that 404 or lead nowhere
  • ‚ùå Missing FDA Disclaimer: Required for ALL health/supplement products
  • ‚ùå No Physical Address: P.O. Box only or no address at all
  • ‚ùå Fake Contact Info: Email or phone that doesn't work
  • ‚ùå No Results Disclaimer: Using testimonials without proper disclosure
  • ‚ùå Outdated Copyright: Using wrong year or wrong company name
  • ‚ùå Hidden Disclaimers: Tiny text or disclaimers users can't find
  • ‚ùå Inconsistent Information: Different addresses/contact info on different pages

‚ö†Ô∏è Consequences of Non-Compliance

Account Suspension: BuyGoods, ClickBank, and other platforms will immediately suspend your account if required elements are missing.
Payment Holds: Payment processors can freeze your funds (sometimes permanently) for compliance violations.
FTC Fines: Federal Trade Commission can fine companies $10,000-$50,000+ per violation for missing disclosures.
Legal Liability: Customers can sue for false advertising, fraud, or misrepresentation if disclosures are missing.
Permanent Bans: Major payment processors share violation data. One ban can blacklist you across all platforms.
Chargebacks: Missing policies increase chargebacks. High chargeback rates = account termination.
State Attorney General Actions: States actively pursue companies with inadequate consumer protections.

9 Implementation Checklist

Follow these steps to ensure full compliance:

  • Step 1: Create complete Privacy Policy (use lawyer or quality template service)
  • Step 2: Create complete Terms & Conditions document
  • Step 3: Create detailed Refund Policy matching your actual policy
  • Step 4: Set up dedicated contact page with all information
  • Step 5: Add footer to EVERY page (VSL, checkout, upsells, downsells, thank you)
  • Step 6: Include FDA disclaimer if selling supplements/health products
  • Step 7: Add results disclaimer near all testimonials and claims
  • Step 8: Verify all links work and go to correct pages
  • Step 9: Test footer displays properly on mobile devices
  • Step 10: Have legal professional review before launch (recommended)

10 Final Critical Reminders

DO NOT SKIP THIS: Legal compliance is not optional and not something to "fix later." Get it right from day one.
  • Every Page Needs Footer: VSL, checkout, all upsells, all downsells, thank you page - no exceptions
  • Links Must Work: Test every link. Broken policy links = compliance violation
  • Keep Current: Update copyright year, review policies annually
  • Match Your Practices: Policies must reflect what you actually do, not generic templates
  • Get Legal Review: Invest in a lawyer reviewing your policies. Cheaper than FTC fines.
  • Monitor Changes: FTC and FDA update requirements. Stay informed.
  • Document Everything: Keep records of when policies were posted and updated

Final Warning

Thousands of vendors lose their accounts every year due to missing compliance elements. Don't let it happen to you. Include EVERY required element on EVERY page before launching.
